== Description ==

Create powerful mini popup surveys and polls for your website with Nimble Squirrel. Gain valuable marketing insights and/or find out how to make your product or service more competitive.

[youtube https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YhU6buCBjBU]

= Features =
* Create mini surveys to help identify how to improve your product or service
* Create a survey to understand your customers needs better
* Create a poll to find out what features your customers want
* Create a popup survey to find out why website visitors are not buying your product
* Gain behavioural insights and voice of customer data
* Create an exit survey to find out why visitors are leaving your site
* Create a website questionnaire to find out which blog posts your readers enjoy
* Many more

> **Please note**
> This plugin requires you to have an account on [Nimble Squirrel](https://nimblesquirrel.com/) to enable you to display a survey on your website.

Once installed, this plugin will allow you to select a specific survey that you would like to display on the front end of your website.

== Installation ==

1. Activate Nimble Squirrel
2. Settings are available in the Settings -> Nimble Squirrel page
4. A checkbox will be added to to your checkout page to allow your customers to subscribe to your newsletters.
